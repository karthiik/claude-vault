## Insights Discovered

### The Long-Distance Challenge

Jamie is in Kansas City. The relationship survives on WhatsApp texts, but FaceTime has fallen off. Text maintains connection; voice/video deepens it.

**Insight:** Daily text is maintenance. Weekly calls are investment.

### Intentional vs. Incidental Time

With Vinay, "being in the same house" isn't the same as "spending time together." The GPA Hero project is great because it's *building* together — active, engaged, teaching.

**Insight:** Schedule the 1:1 time. If it's not blocked, it won't happen.

### Adventures as Relationship Investment

Both relationships benefit from shared adventures:
- Jamie: International trip + national park
- Vinay: Ireland + personal trip + teaching driving

**Insight:** Travel isn't just Joy area — it's relationship investment.
