# Byron Requirements: Global EA Practice Pitch

**Goal:** [[2026 Career Goals|G4: Global EA Practice]] · **Code:** C4

Requirements and guidance from Byron for building the EA Practice proposal.

---

## Core Objective

**Create credibility** — The pitch must establish EA as a legitimate, valuable function.

---

## Key Questions to Answer

1. **What does an EA function look like for Lockton?**
   - How does it work across franchise and regions?
   - What's the operating model?

2. **What's our current maturity?**
   - Where are we today?
   - Honest assessment of capabilities

3. **Business Capabilities & Value**
   - Business value proposition
   - Future portfolio implications

4. **Org Structure Vision (Year 1-3)**
   - What should the org look like?
   - Evolution over time

5. **Knowledge, Skills & Gaps**
   - Current abilities vs. needed abilities
   - How does this evolve?
